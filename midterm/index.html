Life of a Starfish

Life for a house cat involves the usual dose of comfort, curiosity, and playfulness. Cats are independent but strongly attached to their humans, enjoying lounging in warm spots, exploring their environment, and communicating through purring and body language. They thrive in secure environments with interaction from their caregivers.
